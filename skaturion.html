Arena Level Design

The Skaturion project was my first time working on arena-style level design, and it allowed me to explore and learn about areas of level design I had not touched on before. In the whitebox I created, I included:

- A tug-of-war like flow supporting the competitive team-based gameplay;
- Multiple paths of attack and layers of verticality that encourage coordination and teamwork;
- Complex geometry that affords for the fast-paced movement, aided by consistent color-coding;
- A strong visual theme that sold the futuristic sports fantasy.
