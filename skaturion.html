Game Info

Title: Skaturion
Proof of Concept Finished: April 12, 2019
Platforms: Windows
Genre Tags: Multiplayer, Competitive, Team-Based, Class-Based, Sports, Third-Person

Role: Level Designer, Game Designer
Duration: 8 Weeks
Team Size: 6 Designers
Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Skaturion is a multiplayer 3v3 sports game where players, equipped with rollerskates and a rocket booster, fly around the arena at incredibly high speeds and pass the ball around as much as possible; the more passes, the more points will be awarded.

This proof of concept was created in 8 weeks by a team of 6 designers of various disciplines. I was the only level designer on the team and was able to commit fully to this role. Additionally, like all other designers, I was also involved in the design of the game itself, especially in the first week. Lastly, I also recorded and edited the trailer shown above, utilizing UE4's cinematic tools and Adobe Premiere Pro.

The Skaturion project was my first time working on arena-style level design, and it allowed me to explore and learn about areas of level design I had not touched on before. The whitebox I created included:

- A power struggle over the ball and the environment, supporting the competitive team-based gameplay;
- Multiple paths of attack and layers of verticality that encourage coordination and teamwork;
- Complex geometry that affords for the fast-paced movement, aided by consistent color-coding;
- A strong visual theme that sold the futuristic sports fantasy.
