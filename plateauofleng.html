Level Info

Title: Plateau of Leng
Game: Unreal Tournament 4
Finished: November 2, 2018
Gamemode: FFA DeathMatch

Role: Level Designer
Duration: 8 Weeks
Level Editor: Unreal Tournament Editor

Download Link: DM-170388-v07

Plateau of Leng is a whitebox FFA DeathMatch level made for Unreal Tournament. Inspired by the cosmic horror stories of H.P. Lovecraft, this level features a small piece of land ripped from our world and placed in another, with the centerpiece being a church torn apart by tentacles from the sea.

Developed over the course of 8 weeks, this DeathMatch stage ticks all the boxes of a true Unreal Tournament map: a smooth, consistent, and dynamic flow through all the distinct areas, each built around a specific weapon and with at least 3 entry/exit points, and enough secondary paths and "ninja paths".

It took 7 distinct iterations to get to the final whitebox of the level. The changes made in these versions were based on the results of playtesting and collecting feedback constantly. Each iteration tackled one or more areas, either polishing them or scrapping and rebuilding them from scratch.

The level was chosen out of approximately 40 other levels as one of the nine best levels of the year and became open for an art pass because of both its strong, memorable theme and balanced, well-flowing gameplay.

Made for a school project in Year 2, Block A, this was the first project where I did level design full-time, whereas in previous projects I was primarily a technical designer and working with Blueprints in Unreal Engine, and occasionally doing level design whenever I could. However, it was during this block that I truly discovered my passion for level design and made the choice to specialize in the role.
