About Me


I am a student at Breda University of Applied Sciences following the International Game Architecture and Design course in the Netherlands. Starting off as a general game designer and eventually a technical designer, I have since found my way into level design where I have found my passion.

My enthusiasm for game development started when I was young, scripting addons and creating levels for me and my friends to play. This hobby eventually grew into something serious, and I applied for the Game Design course at BUAS, where I am now in my 3rd year.

Reaching the end of my education, I am now looking for an internship. Being both proactive and communicative, I work well in both small and large teams. I am interested in a great variety of genres, but my love is primarily at action-heavy games, single/multiplayer shooters, and (semi-)linear RPGs.
